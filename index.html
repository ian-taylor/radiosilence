About Radio Silence

Although Radio Silence covers songs recorded over several years, most were either recorded or prior recordings were pretty much redone in 2020. While in isolation, I got fed up with social media and decided to remove all apps from my devices and spend weekends more productively and artistically. one song led to another and I ended up recording 14 over a a few months, 12 of which were selected for the album.
